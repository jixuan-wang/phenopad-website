The Problem

Documentation should support care, not pull attention away from it.

For every hour physicians spend on direct patient care, a large amount of additional time goes into administrative work and EHR documentation. PhenoPad was built to make clinical information capture more natural during patient-physician interactions.

The interface lets clinicians write, speak, draw, annotate photos, and review AI-generated suggestions while keeping control over the final note.

Real time Recognition and support update during the visit.
Multi-modal Handwriting, speech, images, drawings, and photos.
Clinician led AI output is presented for validation, not hidden automation.

Core Features

One note-taking surface, four ways to reduce clerical work.

Handwriting Recognition

Write naturally. Review recognized text immediately.

PhenoPad recognizes handwritten lines in real time, shows candidate words, expands abbreviations, and surfaces phenotype tags for quick confirmation.

  • Medical language model for handwritten notes
  • Correction options directly above the writing
  • Phenotype tags with present and absent states
